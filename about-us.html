About Us

Who we are and what we stand for.

Capital Press is an independent digital newsroom focused on clear, timely, and reliable reporting across politics, business, technology, sports, world, health, science, and entertainment.

Our editorial approach is simple: verify facts, cite primary sources when possible, and separate reporting from opinion. We aim to help readers understand what happened, why it matters, and what may happen next.

Our team includes reporters, editors, and producers working across time zones to keep coverage accurate and useful for a global audience.

Our Core Principles

  • Accuracy over speed
  • Context over noise
  • Transparency over speculation
